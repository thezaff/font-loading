What are the fonts?

Popular Formats

  • EOT
  • OTF/TTF
  • WOFF
  • WOFF2

EOT

Supported only in IE. Use if you want support IE below version 9.

No compression by default.

OTF/TTF

Supported by the most browsers and partially by IE. Use to support Androind 4.4 and below.

No compression by default.

WOFF

Supported by almost all browsers.

Built-in compression.

WOFF2

Not supported in IE and some other browsers.

Built-in compression. 30% lighter than WOFF
